Ingredients


* 1 Envelope · Corn starch Maizena brand flavor cajeta

* 2 Cups · Milk

* 1/2 Cup · Sugar

For the cake:

* 3/4 Cup · Flour

* 5 Pieces · Egg

* 4 Tbs. · Sugar

* 1/2 Cup · Skim milk

* 2 Tbsp. · Baking powder Mixture of milks:

* 1 Cup · Condensed milk

* 1 Cup · Evaporated milk

* 1 Cup · Sweet cream

For the cover:

* 2 Cups · Cream to beat

* 2 Tbsp. · Cajeta

Preparation

1. To prepare the cake, separate the whites from the yolks.

2. Beat the egg whites until stiff and reserve. In a separate bowl beat the yolks with the sugar.

3. Add the previously sifted powders to the yolk and sugar mixture. Add milk.

4. Mix the whites in an enveloping way. Empty in 2 molds of 24cm in diameter previously greased and floured. Bake for 25 min at 180 ° C.

5. Prepare the cornstarch azale Cornstarch Maizena® in two cups of milk, heat until it is thick and add the sugar. Let cool completely.

6. Once the bread has been baked, moisten it with the mixture of the milk. Place one bread on a platter, fill with the cold atole and place the other bread previously moistened.

7. Beat the cream of the cover and decorate the cake together with the cajeta.
