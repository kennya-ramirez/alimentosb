Ingredients


* 4 cups of low-fat milk (low-fat)

* 1-2 medium cinnamon slices

* 4 packages of 7 gr. each of natural grenetine without flavor

* 1 cup of cold water

* 1 cup of fat-free sweetened condensed milk (fat-free)

* Berries (or red berries), optional

Preparation

1. Heat the milk with cinnamon over medium heat. Do not lose sight of it because it heats up fast.

2. While the milk is heating, dissolve the grenetina in the cold water cup.

3. When the milk releases the boil, the temperature drops, add the grenetina and dissolve well.

4. Turn off the heat, add the condensed milk and stir until completely integrated. Let cool for 10 minutes or until warm.

5. Strain this mixture in a jar to remove the pieces of cinnamon, then empty the milk into a jelly mold, or into individual glasses.

6. Put in the refrigerator until it is firm. This will take several hours. I recommend that you prepare it the night before so that it is refrigerated all night, or early in the morning of the day you want to serve it.

7. To unmold, with a knife or ice pick carefully separates the gelatin from the wall of the mold. Heat water and empty in a deep container. Dip the mold with the gelatin for a few seconds. Place the plate on top and with a quick and precise movement, turn over. Carefully lift the mold.

8. Serve with berries if you wish.
