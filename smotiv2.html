Start Cambio de Idioma

Ingredients


* 1 cup chopped and chopped spinach

* 1 cup of chopped curly cabbage

* ½ cup of chopped celery

* ½ teaspoon of cinnamon

* 1 green apple, without the heart and chopped

* 1 pear without heart and cut

* 1 frozen banana sliced

* 2 cups of water

* Fresh mint to serve afterwards

preparation

1. Put all the ingredients in a blender and beat until it reaches a smooth texture. This smoothie will make you feel full until lunchtime. Oats and flax seeds provide a good dose of daily fiber, which will give the smoothie a slightly grainy texture.
