Ingredients


* 250 ml. of milk

* 250 ml. of cream

* 25 gr. of cocoa powder

* 85 gr. of chocolate fondant

* 100 gr. of sugar

* 2 egg yolks

* Salt

preparation

1. Beat the yolks with the sugar until it dissolves. book in a bowl.

2. In a saucepan over low heat, put the milk with the cream and cocoa powder. Remove to integrate and dissolve the cocoa.

3. Grate or chop the chocolate fondant, incuborize the previous mixture and beat until the chocolate melts. Add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavor.

4. Incorporate the mixture of yolks that we had reserved, beat the mixture well, leave a few minutes without stopping to stir so that it does not boil and cut. Remove from heat and let temper at room temperature.

5. If we have a refrigerator, cool the mixture and put it in the ice cream maker for about 30 minutes. If not, put in a flat and metallic container, take to the freezer and remove every half hour for three hours to break the ice crystals that are forming.
