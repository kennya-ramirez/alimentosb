Ingredients

* 400 grams of flour.

* 150 grams of butter.

* 30 grams of butter.

* 20 grams of yeast.

* Half a glass of milk.

* 3 eggs.

* A pinch of salt.

* Oil for frying.

* Sugar for decoration.

Preparation

1. In a large bowl or on a counter put the flour and form a crown, in the center add the yeast, milk and a small amount of sugar. Knead everything until a dough forms.

2. Then add the remaining sugar, butter that should be room temperature, eggs and pinch of salt. Integrate all the ingredients and knead well, then leave the flour in a container covered with a cloth and let it sit for an hour.

3. After resting time, cover a counter or work surface with flour and there spread the dough with the help of a roller, should be 8 mm thick. Then cut the donuts with a round cutter and inside each donut cut with a smaller one to make the characteristic hole. Put the donuts in a tray, cover and let them rest in a warm place for 2 hours.

4. Next, put oil in a pan and when it is hot add the donuts, when they have browned on both sides remove and drain, put on absorbent paper to remove the remaining oil.

5. Finally, cover all the donuts with enough sugar and share, the diners will want each time more and more.
