Ingredients

* 2 1/4 teaspoons of yeast

* 180 ml water tivia

* 1/3 cup of sugar

* 1/2 teaspoon salt

* 1/4 cup butter

* 1 egg

* 1 yolk

* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

* 3 cups of flour

* 4 cups of oil

Preparation

1. COMBINE the yeast with the water in a bowl and let stand for 10 minutes.

2. ADD sugar, salt, butter, egg, yolk, vanilla extract and flour. Knead in a blender slowly until it becomes a homogenous dough.

3. TRANSFER the dough into an oiled bowl and cover with plastic in a warm place for 2 hours.

4. PRESS the dough to deflate, wrap in plastic and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.

5. ENHARINE your work surface and spread the dough until it is about half an inch thick. Use a circular cookie cutter or floured donuts. Cut the donuts and let them rest covered with a clean kitchen towel in a baking tray for 1 hour.

6. HEAT oil in a pot and fry one by one. More or less 1 minute on each side or until they look golden brown. Drain in a grate with absorbent paper.

Glazed

* 2 cups of icing sugar

* 1/4 cup of water

* 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Preparation

1. MIX the icing sugar, the water, the vanilla until everything is well incorporated.

2. REMOVE EACH donut in the mixture and return to the grid. Let the glaze dry for 15 minutes and serve.

* If you need to reheat the donuts you can put them 8 seconds in the microwave.
