For the filling:

* 2 cooked chicken breasts

* 1 kilo of tomato

* 1 onion

* 100 grams of Parmesan cheese

* 2 cans of half cream

* 1 poblano chile

Preparation

1. Mix all the ingredients for the dough of the crepes and make them in a pan.

2. Blend the tomato with poblano chili and fry with a little oil.

3. Arrange a layer of crepes and place a layer of breast, tomato sauce, cheese, rajas until you finish using all the ingredients.

4. Bake only for gratin.
