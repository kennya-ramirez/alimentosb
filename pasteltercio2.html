Start Cambio de Idioma

Ingredients

For the cake:

* 2 1/2 cups of flour

* 1 tablespoon of salt

* 1/4 cup of cocoa

* 1 1/2 cups of sugar

* 1 1/2 cups of vegetable oil

* 2 eggs

* 1/4 cup of red dye

* 1 tablespoon of vanilla essencea * 1 cup of milk

* 3 tablespoons of white vinegar

* 1 1/2 tablespoons of baking soda

For cream cheese bitumen:

* 2 sticks of unsalted butter

* 340 grams of cream cheese

* 4 cups of sugar glass

* 3/4 tablespoon of vanilla essence

Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees centigrade.

2. Beat the sugar and the oil.

3. Add the eggs one by one and beat well each time you put one.

4. Add the red coloring together with the vanilla and continue beating.

5. Gradually add the flour, cocoa, milk, salt and 1 tablespoon of white vinegar. It is beaten well until all ingredients are incorporated.

6. In a deep, small bowl, mix the remaining vinegar with the baking soda, add to the dough and continue whisking for 10 minutes.

7. Grease with unsalted butter, two round baking pans and distribute the mixture in equal parts.

8. Bake for 30 min or until inserting a toothpick comes out clean.

9. Remove the cake from the oven and allow it to cool before unmolding.

10. Spread the top of one of the cakes with the cream cheese bitumen and put the other one on top to cover them completely with the same bitumen.

Preparation for cream cheese bitumen:

Beat butter and cream cheese until fluffy.

Add the glass sugar and vanilla to continue whisking for 5 more minutes, until there is a kind of soft paste to spread.
