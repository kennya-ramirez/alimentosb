Ingredients


* 2 tablespoons of instant coffee (cappuccino type)

* 120 ml of milk

* 2 tablespoons of sugar

* 6 ice cubes

* Chantilly cream to decorate on top

preparation

1. Put all the ingredients in the blender and process until it is like a frozen cream (frozen).

2. Serve in a tall glass and garnish with whipped cream over, grated chocolate, some flavored syrup taste, strawberry, cinnamon or whatever you like.
