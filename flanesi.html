Ingredients


For the flan:

* 5 eggs

* 1 can (12oz) of evaporated milk

* 1 can (14oz) of condensed milk

* 1 tablespoon of vanilla

* 1/2 cup of sugar

* For the caramel:

* 1 cup of sugar

* 2 tablespoons of water

Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 350 Â° F and have the mold you will use for the bain-marie on hand.

2. Place the sugar for the caramel in a skillet over medium heat with the 2 tablespoons of water until it melts evenly.

3. Cook until the candy acquires a golden color.

4. Pour it into the mold you will use. Spread the candy carefully through the mold until it covers the walls of the mold. Let it cool.

5. Add the eggs and liquefy only for a few seconds. Then add the evaporated milk and mix. Incorporate condensed milk and mix. Add vanilla, sugar and continue mixing until you get a homogeneous mixture.

6. Strain and pour the mixture into the mold you have previously caramelized.

7. Take the oven to a bain-marie for 45 minutes.

8. Verify that you are ready using a skewer stick or a fine knife. If they come clean, it means that you are ready.

9. Take it out of the oven with great care not to burn yourself and put it on a wire rack. Allow to cool to room temperature until it is completely cold.

10. Unmold and take to the refrigerator until the moment of serving.
