Ingredients


* 1 ½ kg of flour that yields 90 cookies

* 1 kg of sugar glass.

* 1 kg of shortening.

* 2 tablespoons of liquid vanilla.

* 200 g of glass sugar.

Preparation

1. Place the sugar and butter on the board or table.

2. Carve the two ingredients until are well incorporated.

3. Add vanilla and little flour little by little to form a soft paste.

4. Flour the board or table where they are going to place the pasta.

5. Spread the dough gently with the hands and later with the roller.

6. Cut circles.

7. Place 12 pieces in each tray.

8. To bake the polvorones requires a temperature of 150 ° to 100 ° in baking oven or pumpkin.

9. Put the trays on the opposite side where they lit the fire and left cook the polvorones during 15 minutes. Place them on the edges since there is less heat in those areas of the furnace.

10. Check the bread at 10 minutes and turn the trays so that it cooks evenly ...

11. Take out the bread when you buy a bone color or well cooked.

Note: You can test if the bread is well cooked placing your finger on the surface, if hard or has consistency when pressed, it means that it is well cooked.

12. Remove the trays with the help of Rags and shovel and let the bread cool.

13. Sprinkle the polvorones with sugar glass with the help of the sieve or colander.

14. Place the polvorones in clean trays.
