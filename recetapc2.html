Start suggestions Cambio de Idioma

Ingredients

* 200 grams of flour + the necessary to knead

* 1 tablespoon of salt

* 1 spoon of sugar

* 1 egg

* 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil

* 1 tablespoon of yeast

* 75 ml of whole milk, at room temperature

preparation

1. PLACE flour, salt and sugar in a bowl. Add the oil, the yeast, the milk and the egg.

2. MIX with a spatula and when the ingredients are well integrated, knead for 6 minutes on a clean surface. Let it rest in a warm place for an hour or until it doubles its volume.

3. PUT the dough to remove the air that accumulates, stretch and fold the dough inward along and then across. Place in a rectangular mold and let stand for half an hour.

4. BAKE at 180 Â° C for 25 minutes or until the bread is golden brown.

5. Allow to cool completely and portion or reserve. Keep in a plastic bag so it does not dry out.

You can add sunflower seeds, linseed, aromatic herbs and the spices that you like the most to achieve a unique bread.
